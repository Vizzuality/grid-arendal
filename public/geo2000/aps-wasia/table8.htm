Alternative Policy Study: Water Resource Management in West Asia

Table 8: Water Balance in West Asia(1)
Scenario II: Supply Augmentation

Assumptions:

  • Available water resources are developed as follows:
  • Groundwater at 100 Mm3 a year, i.e. 2 000 Mm3 for each sub-region by 2015.
  • Desalination plant capacity is increased gradually to reach 3 000 Mm3 in the Arabian Peninsula by 2015.
  • Wastewater plant capacity is increased to reach 3 000 Mm3 in the Arabian Peninsula and 2 000 Mm3 in the Mashriq by 2015.
  • UN projection of population (1997) is applied.
  • Domestic and industrial consumption are secured.
  • Rates of water consumption are as shown in box 1.
  • Improved agricultural productivity will achieve 17 per cent saving in agricultural water demand by 2015 (see box 1).
  • Shared water resource disputes are settled.
