About this web-site

This web-site contains the full text and graphics from five special reports by the IPCC's Working Groups. It was first released for the 6th Conference of the Parties to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change in The Hague, The Netherlands, 13-24 November 2000. This web-site is the second edition, produced with the assistance of UNEP/GRID-Arendal.
