This summary was accepted but not approved in detail at the Sixth Session of IPCC Working Group III (Accra, Ghana · 28 February - 3 March 2001). "Acceptance" of IPCC reports at a session of the Working Group or Panel signifies that the material has not been subject to line-by-line discussion and agreement, but nevertheless presents a comprehensive, objective, and balanced view of the subject matter.
