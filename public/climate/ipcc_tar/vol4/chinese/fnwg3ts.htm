6 Reserves are those occurrences that are identified and measured as economically and technically recoverable with current technologies and prices. On top of that there are additional quantities with unknown certainty of occurrence and/or with unknown or no economic significance in the foreseeable future, referred to as "additional occurrences" (SAR). Examples of unconventional fossil fuel resources are tar sands and shale oils, geo-pressured gas, and gas in aquifers.
