About this website

This website contains the three working group contributions to the IPCC Third Assessment Report "Climate Change 2001" in English. It also contains the Synthesis Report of the IPCC Third Assessment Report and its Summary for Policymakers and the Summaries for Policymakers and Technical Summaries of the working grosup contributions in English, Arabic, Chinese, French, Russian and Spanish.
