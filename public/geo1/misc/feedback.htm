We sincerely appreciate feedback from the users of GEO-1 on Internet. We would, however, appreciate that your feedback is directed to the right addressee. Therefore:

Feedback on the content of the GEO-1 report:

Please, send an e-mail to the GEO-1 Project team (geo@unep.org) at the UNEP HQ in Nairobi, Kenya. Be as concise, specific and brief as possible. We expect comments from many users of the GEO-1 report. Please, therefore, accept our apologies for not being able to respond back to all of you, but do be assured that we will be using your feedback.

Feedback on the GEO-1 on Internet:

Please, send an e-mail to the UNEP/GRID-Arendal (grid@grida.no) , responsible for the implementation of the Internet version, if you have comments about the Web version. If the comments consider specific Web pages, please indicate the page address (URL).
