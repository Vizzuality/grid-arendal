The North American Waterfowl Management Plan publicizes its activities and accomplishments regularly. The newsletter Birdscapes updates readers on habitat restoration activities of Plan partners. The Winter 1999/2000 issue includes a center pullout that highlights the progress of all of the Plan's Joint Venture's in North America. Every four years Plan partners produce an Update to the Plan. The 1998 Update to the North American Waterfowl Management Plan, Expanding the Vision (2.4 MB), outlines the Plan's central mandate and its revised population and habitat goals. Plan coordinators regularly produce brochures, news releases, backgrounders, and articles on waterfowl habitat conservation activities and on the Plan's progress.
