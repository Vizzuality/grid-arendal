Contributors to the report
This report is the product of co-operative work by most technical units of FAO. It
was prepared by a team led by J. Bruinsma under the general direction of H. de Haen,
Assistant Director-General, Economic and Social Department. Members of the team were
N. Alexandratos (consultant), G. Bödeker and M.G. Ottaviani. E. Kueneman (assisted by
D. Tiwari) co-ordinated contributions of the Agriculture Department. T. Aldington
(consultant) provided comments on Chapters 4 to 8.
Main contributors to or authors of the individual Chapters were as follows: Chapter
1 (Introduction and overview): J. Bruinsma and N. Alexandratos. Chapter 2 (Prospects for
food and nutrition): N. Alexandratos with inputs from L. Naiken of the Statistics Division
(estimates of chronic undernourishment) and J. Cotier of the Food and Nutrition Division
(diets). Chapter 3 (Prospects for agriculture and major commodity groups): N. Alexandratos
with inputs from A. Gürkan, M. Mielke, C. Calpe, N. Morgan and P. Thoenes of the
Commodities and Trade Division (commodity projections), S. Mohanty of FAPRI
(prospects for India) and G. Scott of CIP (prospects for roots and tubers). Chapter 4
(Growth in crop production): J. Bruinsma with inputs from F. Nachtergaele (agricultural
land potential), J.M. Faurès and J. Hoogeveen (irrigation), and J. Poulisse (fertilizers) of
the Land and Water Development Division, and D. Tran, J. Marathee, P. Griffee and V.
Nguyen (crop land and yield projections) of the Plant Production and Protection Division.
Chapter 5 (Livestock production): S. Mack with inputs from H. Steinfeld (animal
production projections), both of the Animal Production and Health Division. Chapter 6
(Forestry): Qiang Ma and C. Nair of the Forestry Policy and Planning Division, who co-
ordinated the contribution from the Forestry Department. Chapter 7 (Fisheries): U.
Wijkstrom of the Fishery Policy and Planning Division, who co-ordinated the contributions
of specialists in the Fisheries Department. Chapter 8 (Environmental aspects of natural
resource use in agriculture): D. Norse (consultant) with inputs from J. Tschirley and L.
Collette of the Research, Extension and Training Division, T. Friedrich of the Agricultural
Support Systems Division and H. Steinfeld of the Animal Production and Health Division.
G. Fischer (International Institute for Applied Systems Analysis), H. van Velthuizen
(consultant) together with F. Nachtergaele were the main authors of the Global Agro-
Ecological Zones Assessment used in this study and M. van `t Riet (consultant) processed
the results of this assessment.
M.G. Ottaviani was responsible for most of the data preparation, statistical analysis
and the Appendices. I. Reyes provided statistical assistance. P. Mascianà was responsible
for the manuscript preparation.
