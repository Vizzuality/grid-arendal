Did you know?
The 44 States listed in Annex 2 to the CTBT have either nuclear research or nuclear power reactors. Each of these States must sign and ratify the Treaty before it can enter into force. So far 41 Annex 2 States have signed the Treaty, and 31 of them have ratified it.
Conference on Facilitating the Entry into Force of the CTBT


Vienna, Austria, 17 October 2001
New dates for the Conference on Facilitating the Entry into Force of the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty have been agreed, so that the Conference can take place in parallel to the rescheduled General Debate of the current 56th session of the United Nations General Assembly. The Conference will be held at UN Headquarters in New York from 11 to 13 November 2001.

As originally envisaged, the Conference will last for three days, the time of the closure session to be determined according to the list of speakers.
