Diamonds

BHP Billiton produces three to four million carats of rough gem quality diamonds annually, supplying nearly four per cent of current world diamond production by weight and six per cent by value. Diamonds from the mine are sold to key customers and on the open market. BHP Billiton also markets high-quality polished stones through its EKATI™ and AURIAS™ branding initiatives.

Operations
Diamonds are sourced from the Ekati Diamond Mine™, located in the Northwest Territories, Canada. The project has been developed in an isolated area, 200 kilometres south of the Arctic Circle and 300 kilometres northeast of Yellowknife, the nearest major community. Access is by air only, except during a three-month period in winter, when a 400-kilometre ice road allows for the trucking of bulk supplies to the site.

The mine plan envisages mining five kimberlite pipes by open-pit methods, two of which will be later mined by underground methods as well. Three more pipes are in the process of being permitted and, in total, 138 kimberlite pipes have been identified on the property (the majority, however, are not economic).

The first kimberlite pipe was discovered in 1991. Site construction started in 1996 and production began in October 1998. Since then, over $US1 billion of rough diamonds have been produced and sold.

Marketing
Diamonds are sorted in Yellowknife and Antwerp, and then sold through a multi-channel marketing program — over 55% into the open market in Antwerp (Belgium), up to 10% to three manufacturing factories in Yellowknife and 35% to De Beers. A strong and loyal customer base has been developed in the major cutting and polishing centres throughout the world, notably Israel, Belgium and India. Our involvement with De Beers began in 1999, when we signed a three-year agreement for the sale of run-of-mine production from the Ekati Diamond Mine™. Regular sales to De Beers began the following year.  

Polished diamonds programs
From selected high-quality stones, we have arrangements for the polishing of stones for two branded diamond initiatives — EKATI™ and AURIAS™.

EKATI™ diamonds are sold through selected retailers in Canada and the US. Each stone is accompanied by a certificate confirming it has originated from the Ekati Diamond Mine™.

AURIAS™ diamonds are sold through selected retailers in Australia and Asia, as well as on the Internet via our AURIAS™ online retail store.
