What is Forest Resources Assessment?  

Forest Resources Assessment is about predicting the potential supply of goods and services from the forest.

Forestry information requirements are multi-dimensional and include parameters relevant to productive, environmental and socio-economic benefits from and in the forest. The challenge is to improve the management of forests by providing decision makers and stakeholders with the best possible, most relevant and cost-effective information for their purpose at the local, national and international levels.

Without comprehensive information on the status, dynamics and responses of the forest ecosystem, it is impossible to evaluate management strategies. This is true not only at the local, operational level, but across broader expanses as well, since consequences of forest practices often extend beyond national boundaries and thus become global concerns.
