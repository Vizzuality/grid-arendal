(1) Urban & Rural Definitions:

1- Urban: Consists of cities and population agglomerations exceeding 2500 persons. The exception is the Indsterial Area & Awali Town which have been considered Urban through the population size of each is less than 2500.

 

2- Rural: Consists of villages and population agglomerations with less than 2500 persons.

(2) All towns are considered Urban places in 1965 census. although Urban places are not defined In 1971 Census, towns of more than 1500 persons were considered Urban places

(3) Includes Population Resident in Hawar Island.

(4) Other Islands Population Re-distributed Propor-tionately Among Main Islands Regions.

(5) Bahrainis Abroad Has Been Enumerated Accord-ing to Region With The Rest of Their Own Families Which They Are Still Inside The Country.
