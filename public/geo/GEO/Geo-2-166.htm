Fertilizer requirements in 2015 and 2030


July 2000, 38 pp
ISBN 92-5-104450-3
Price: US$8.00.

The specific objective of this paper is to explore the bounds on the amount of fertilizer needed to support FAO projections of agricultural commodity production for 2015 and 1030.This study represents an unprecedented co-ordinated effort, involving both the private and the public sectors, in order to arrive at a result which is of fundamental importance for world agriculture and for the mineral fertilizer industry.
