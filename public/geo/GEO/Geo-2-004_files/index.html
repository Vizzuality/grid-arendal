WWF leads international efforts to protect the diversity of life on earth. The Climate Change Campaign is working in over 25 countries to raise awareness of the threats of climate change to wildlife and natural ecosystems, pressure governments into taking action to prevent climate change, and to build partnerships with business and industry to find solutions to the problem.
